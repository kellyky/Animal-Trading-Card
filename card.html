Feral Cat

The feral kitten pictured lives at Wai-O-Tapu with his mom and siblings.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: 18 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 2 - 16 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands, tundra, coastal areas, agricultural land, scrublands, urban areas, and wetlands

Feral cats are domestic cats who've reverted to the wild or who were born in the wild. They're typically about 18 inches long and weigh about 8-10 lbs.
